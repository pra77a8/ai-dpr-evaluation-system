DETAILED PROJECT REPORT (DPR)
================================

Project Title: Rural Road Development - Phase 2

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This project aims to improve connectivity in rural areas by constructing and upgrading road infrastructure. The project will enhance transportation facilities for local communities and support economic development in the region.

2. BUDGET DETAILS
Total Project Cost: ₹30,00,000
Fund Allocation: Government Grant - ₹30,00,000
Estimated Expenditure Breakdown:
- Materials: ₹15,00,000
- Labor: ₹10,00,000
- Equipment: ₹3,00,000
- Contingency: ₹2,00,000

3. TIMELINE
Project Duration: 18 months
Commencement Date: January 2025
Completion Target: June 2026
Key Milestones:
- Site Preparation: January - February 2025 (2 months)
- Foundation Work: March - June 2025 (4 months)
- Road Construction: July 2025 - February 2026 (8 months)
- Finishing & Landscaping: March - May 2026 (3 months)
- Final Inspection: June 2026 (1 month)

6. ENVIRONMENTAL CONCERNS
Environmental Impact Assessment:
- Minimal tree cutting required (less than 50 trees)
- Flood-prone area during monsoon season (July-September)
- Soil erosion prevention measures to be implemented
- Drainage system designed to handle heavy rainfall
- Wildlife corridor preservation in northern section
Mitigation Measures:
- Reforestation program post-completion
- Silt traps installation
- Controlled water discharge system
- Regular environmental monitoring

Road Length: 12.5 kilometers
Road Width: 7 meters (carriageway) + 2x1.5 meters (shoulder)
Surface Material: Bituminous concrete
Base Course: Crushed stone aggregates
Drainage System: Culverts and side drains
Safety Features: Guardrails, signage, and lighting
Quality Standards: IRC:58-2011, IRC:37-2018
